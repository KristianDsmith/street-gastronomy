About this book

20 years ago, I was a freshman in a restaurant kitchen, a guy who had just finished cooking school in London. That was when my Jamaican dad brought the whole family to the island for the first time. Once there, we didn't end up among the tourists' luxurious hotel complexes - dad showed us the real Jamaica. Living and eating with the locals made me discover my Jamaican roots. The smokey aroma of burning pimento in grills packed with jerk chicken, the spicy fish dishes down by the beach, the pride and joy of cooking - all of which I convey in this book Jamaican food culture is a result of the island's exciting history, and I have made it my mission to spread all the knowledge I have managed to gather.
In Roots, you learn the secrets behind authentic Jamaican cooking.
